Hunza Valley

Hunza is one of Pakistan's most breathtaking mountain valleys, nestled in Gilgit-Baltistan at an altitude of 2,438 metres.

Key Attractions

Rakaposhi Viewpoint

Rakaposhi is the 27th highest mountain in the world. The viewpoint at Minapin village offers a stunning panoramic view of the peak.

Attabad Lake

Formed after a 2010 landslide, Attabad Lake's brilliant turquoise waters are a striking natural wonder and a boating hotspot.

Baltit Fort

An 800-year-old fort overlooking Karimabad. A UNESCO heritage candidate offering panoramic views of the valley.
