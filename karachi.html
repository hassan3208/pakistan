Karachi - City of Lights

Karachi is Pakistan's largest city and economic hub, home to over 20 million people and a stunning Arabian Sea coastline.

Top Attractions

Clifton Beach

A popular seafront destination for locals and tourists, featuring camel rides, food stalls, and beautiful sunsets over the Arabian Sea.

Mohatta Palace

A stunning example of Sindhi and Rajasthani architecture built in 1927. Now a museum displaying art, history, and cultural heritage.

Pakistan Maritime Museum

Home to decommissioned warships and submarines, offering an immersive look into Pakistan's naval history.
