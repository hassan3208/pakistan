Swat – Switzerland of Pakistan

Swat Valley in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa is known for its lush green meadows, alpine forests, and crystal-clear rivers.

Top Attractions

Malam Jabba Ski Resort

Pakistan's only ski resort, Malam Jabba offers skiing in winter and chairlift rides with panoramic mountain views year-round.

Mahodand Lake

A stunning glacial lake at 3,600 metres, surrounded by meadows and snow-capped peaks. Best visited from June to September.

Mingora City

The main city of Swat, known for its emerald gemstone market, archaeological museum, and as a base for valley exploration.
